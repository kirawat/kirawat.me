Macbook Air M1

Planted  21 Dec 2023 10:35:00 AM (+07:00)
Last tended  21 Dec 2023 10:37:23 AM (+07:00)

The M1 chip is very efficient and generates a relatively little heat, and the MacBook Air’s aluminium chassis helps to dissipate heat. Additionally, the MacBook Air has built-in thermal protection that will automatically reduce performance if the internal temperature gets too high.

If MacBook Air does overheat, it will automatically shut down to prevent damage.
