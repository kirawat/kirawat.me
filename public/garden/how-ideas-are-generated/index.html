How Ideas are Generated?

Planted  19 Dec 2023 13:46:00 PM (+07:00)
Last tended  20 Dec 2023 10:46:54 AM (+07:00)

A magician can’t pulls something out of a hat unless they put something in there first.

Ink & Switch

The ideas aren’t summoned from nowhere: they come from raw material, other ideas or observations about the world. Hence a two-step creative process: collect raw material, then think about it. From this process comes pattern recognition and eventually the insights that form the basis of novel ideas.1
